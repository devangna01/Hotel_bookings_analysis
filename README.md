# Hotel_bookings_analysis
Comprehensive data analysis of hotel booking trends and cancellation behaviors to provide actionable business recommendations
Hotel Booking Data Analysis | TravClan AssignmentAuthor: Devangna Project OverviewThis project involves a deep dive into hotel booking datasets to identify patterns in customer behavior, booking channels, and revenue leakage due to cancellations. The goal is to provide data-driven strategies to improve conversion quality and optimize revenue.Key InsightsChannel Dominance: Digital channels (Web ~50% and Mobile App ~40%) contribute nearly 90% of total bookings.Room Demand: Customers prioritize affordability, with the majority of demand concentrated in standard and mid-tier rooms.Cancellation Crisis: The overall cancellation rate is ~20% (1 in 5 bookings), indicating significant revenue leakage regardless of the booking channel.Seasonality: Booking values peak in early months (Feb-March) and dip mid-year.Technical StackLanguage: PythonLibraries: Pandas (Data Manipulation), Matplotlib & Seaborn (Visualization).Tools: Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab.Business RecommendationsBased on the analysis, the following strategies were proposed:Reduce Revenue Leakage: Introduce tiered cancellation penalties and promote non-refundable bookings with incentives.Optimize Channel Strategy: Focus marketing spend on high-scalability digital channels (Web & Mobile).Improve Conversion: Use behavioral nudges (reminders and urgency messaging) to increase booking commitment.
